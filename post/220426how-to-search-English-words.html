How to Search English Words

本文最后更新于：3 个月前

检索单词方法

  1. Googling “ + camb” to get the definition in the Cambridge English Dictionary
  2. Googling “ + youglish” to see pronunciations in English from ytb.
  3. Googling “ + vs” to see the difference between words.
  4. You can also deepen your memory through pictures.
